Resource Road Multi-Vehicle Classifier

Instructions

Click the Select Image button below and choose a vehicle image from your computer to upload. Sample images can be downloaded from the following Google Drive folder.
After selecting and uploading an image, click the Analyze button. When the image analysis is complete, the predicted vehicle classification will be displayed below the button.

Classifications

The ten possible vehicle classifications and are listed below along with a short description for each:

  • log-truck-loaded: loaded log truck
  • log-truck-empty: empty log truck
  • log-truck-dark: log trucks before sunrise
  • forestry-other: chip trucks, low-beds, gravel trucks, etc.
  • grader: road grader
  • industrial-commercial: large industrial or commerciale vehicles (non-forestry) such as tractor trailers, fuel trucks, buess, BC Hydro trucks, flatbeds, etc.
  • passenger: cars, pick-ups (including forestry crew trucks), vans, SUVs, pickups w/ trailer, etc.
  • passenger-dark: passenger vehicles before sunrise or after sunset
